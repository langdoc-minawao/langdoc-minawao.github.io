If you are interested in knowing more about this sub-project, you can do the following:
1. **check out our [IIAB GitHub repo](https://github.com/langdoc-minawao/iiab-outreach)**, where we publish updates about our modules and the current developemnt we are doing;
2. **get direct access to our IIAB servers in Minawao**, so that you can see our content and module structure for yourself. We would send you an email to install [Tailscale](https://tailscale.com) and get into our tailnet. So, feel free to send us an email at [pierpaolo.dicarlo@unior.it](mailto:pierpaolo.dicarlo@unior.it)!
